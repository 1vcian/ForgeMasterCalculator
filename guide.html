Game Basics

‚è∞ When does the game reset?

Daily reset at midnight UTC. This includes dungeon keys, challenge tickets, and war days.

üì¥ What's the offline timer?

Base max is 4 hours for idle hammer & coin collection.

‚úèÔ∏è Can I change my IGN?

First change is free, subsequent changes cost 200 gems. Names must be unique across all servers.

üåê How are servers assigned?

You're placed with players who started in the same period in your region. No server transfers.

Dungeon Unlocks

Hammer Thief: Unlocks at 2-10

Ghost Town: Unlocks at 2-15

Invasion: Unlocks at 3-1

Zombie Rush: Unlocks at 4-1

Dungeon Keys

üîë Keys reset at midnight and are only used if you beat the level

üìä Key rollover: 0, 1, or 2 keys ‚Üí reset to 2/2. Keys over 2 remain until used.

Use "Sweep Last" to auto-complete your highest level when stuck.

Game Terms

META: Most Effective Tactics Available

IGN: In-Game Name

DPS: Damage Per Second

LB: Leaderboard

Stat Definitions

Attack Speed: Increases how often you attack

Block Chance: Chance to block an attack

Critical Chance: Chance to deal a critical hit

Critical Damage: Bonus damage on critical attacks

Double Chance: Chance to hit twice in a row

Health Regen: Auto-heal per second, based on max HP

Lifesteal: Heal per weapon attack, based on damage

Skill Cooldown: Time for skills to reactivate
