Tech Tree Overview

The tree is split into 3 main branches: Forge, Power, and Skills/Pets

Each node can be upgraded 5√ó per tier, for a total of 25 upgrades per node

With each tier, cost and time increase, while bonus values stay the same

Push several nodes to Tier II before specializing ‚Äî this gives a smoother early game power curve.

Forge Tree

Forge Timer: +4% upgrade speed per point

Forge Upgrade Cost: -2% forge upgrade cost per point

Equipment Sell Price: +2% revenue per sale per point

Hammer Thief Hammer Bonus: +2% hammer drops per point

Hammer Thief Coin Bonus: +2% coin drops per point

Auto Forge: +1 additional hammer at a time (1√ó per tier)

Free Forge Chance: +1% chance to forge for free per point

Max Offline Time: +16% max offline duration per point

Coin Offline Reward: +2% passive Coin gain per minute

Hammer Offline Reward: +2% passive Hammer gain per minute

Prioritize this tree early to speed up upgrades and boost offline progress.

Power Tree

Weapon/Glove/Necklace/Ring Mastery: +2% damage per point

Helmet/Body/Shoe/Belt Mastery: +2% health per point

Equipment Level Up (all 8 slots): +2 max upgrade level per point

Mount Damage Mastery: +2% mount damage per point

Mount Health Mastery: +2% mount health per point

Mount Summon Cost: -1% Clock Winders needed per point

Extra Mount Chance: +2% chance to spawn two mounts per point

A late-game tree once your forge is efficient.

Substat Effects

Damage: Increases all weapon and skill damage

Health: Increases your base health by x%

Ranged Damage: Only affects ranged weapons

Melee Damage: Only affects melee weapons (useless without one equipped)

Skill Damage: Boosts skill power and buff skill HP and damage

Health Regen: Restores HP every second, strong early on

Lifesteal: Converts a percentage of weapon damage into HP (pairs with Attack Speed and Double Chance)

Attack Speed: Faster attacks mean more hits and more lifesteal

Double Chance: Chance for main weapon to strike twice

Crit Chance: Chance to land a critical hit (base 120% damage)

Crit Damage: Raises crit multiplier, combine with Crit Chance

Block: Chance to block incoming hits

Ranged weapons generally outperform melee unless you have specific bonuses.

Recommended Builds

Balanced Setup (All-Round)

  • Attack Speed 100% or more
  • Lifesteal 40% or more
  • Double Chance 80% or more
  • Damage

Endgame Skill Build

  • Full Skill Damage
  • Great with Legendary or higher skills and cooldown reduction
  • Fun build but gets outclassed by speed/steal/double/crit in endgame

Endgame Crit Build

  • Crit Chance and Crit Damage combo
  • Best once dual-substat gear and pets are unlocked

Experiment freely ‚Äî each combo shines in different stages of progression.
